About Davis Firefighters Local 3494

We are proud members of the Davis Fire Department, dedicated to protecting and serving the Davis community. Local 3494 represents the professional firefighters of Davis, California. Our members are committed to excellence in emergency response, community service, and public education.

Learn More About Us

Our Leadership

President

Matt has served as the President of Local 3494 since 2020. He has been with the Davis Fire Department for 15 years and is committed to representing the interests of our firefighters.

Local 3494 Honor Guard

The Local 3494 Honor Guard serves at ceremonies, memorial services, and special events, representing the tradition and dignity of the fire service. Members of our Honor Guard are trained in ceremonial procedures and protocols.
